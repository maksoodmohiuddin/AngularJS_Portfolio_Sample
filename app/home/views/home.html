Helpful people deserve rewards

We're on a mission here at Xbox to reward those who are helpful, insightful, and passionate about our product. Those in the community that give back by leading, supporting, motivating and contributing deserve recognition.

How it works

SPECIALIZATIONS

Find something you're particularly passionate about, and enlist to be a part of that specialization.

SEASONS

With each new season, new specializations and missions become available. Compare yourself against your friends season over season, and strive to be the season winner!

TEIRS

Each season grants an Ambassador the ability to reach the top teir for that season; each teir unlocking new rewards and activities.

MISSIONS

Check back often for custom missions in each specialization that grant more rewards.

COMPETE

Out do one another in helping the community and product. Compare your day to day stats with your friends as you rise up the leaderboard!
